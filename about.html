About Naruto

Naruto Uzumaki was born the day a giant demon fox, the Nine-Tailed Fox (Kurama), attacked his village.
To stop it, the Fourth Hokage sealed the beast inside baby Naruto.
Because of this:
Naruto grows up as a jinchūriki (a human with a tailed beast inside)
The villagers fear and reject him
He doesn’t understand why he’s hated So Naruto makes it his goal to become Hokage, the strongest ninja and leader of the village, so everyone will finally acknowledge him.
